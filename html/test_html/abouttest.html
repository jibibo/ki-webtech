OUR STORY

We founded Uvazon at the heart of innovation and science, namely at Science Park Amsterdam.

Our main goal was to provide services for students to make their lives more flexible and easy.

Since we are students ourselves, we could understand a student's needs as no other.

Therefore we could no longer standby and we took immediately matters into our own hands.

After months of hard work and sleepless nights, we now present to you the solution of our future: Uvazon.

OUR MISSION

We want to bring joy and inspiration to every student in the world.

In other words: to every human being in the world, because everyone is a student of life.

With everything that Uvazon has to offer, we hope to put a smile on people's face.

With our innovative services and our educational character, we hope to bring more color into the world.

We hope to be a place where we can always be there for one another.

OUR FUTURE

In the future we hope to create a more accessible and dynamic platform.

Where our services could be accessed worldwide.

Where we can create an outstanding user experience.

Where we can grow together as a community.

And where we can bring Uvazon closer to you.
